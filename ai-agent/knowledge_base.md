# DealLock — Knowledge Base

## What is DealLock?

DealLock is a Nigerian buy-now-pay-later platform that helps users secure products
at good prices even when they don't have the full amount available right now.

When you find a product you want to buy — on Jumia, Jiji, Konga, Instagram,
Facebook Marketplace, or elsewhere — you submit it to DealLock. If the deal is
approved, DealLock completes the purchase by fronting the remaining balance you
don't yet have. You then repay DealLock in weekly installments over 1 or 2 weeks. Once payment is complete, the product is delivered to you.

---

## How does the deal process work?

1. You submit a deal on your dashboard: product name, link, seller details,
   delivery address, item photo, your deposit amount, and your preferred
   installment duration.
2. DealLock's AI engine analyses the deal — checking price fairness, scam risk,
   and how resellable the item is. This takes a few minutes.
3. You receive a result: Approved, Flagged for review, or Rejected.
4. If approved, DealLock completes the payment to the seller 
5. The product is held in escrow until You  repay DealLock in weekly installments per the agreed schedule.
6. Once payment is complete, The item is delivered to your delivery address.
Notes:
1. Default in payment completion would lead to the item/product being resold. 
2. A user can request for a time extension to complete payments but exceeding deadline would incur additional charges.
3. The customer bears all delivery costs.

---

## What products can I submit?

You can submit almost any physical product — electronics, fashion, appliances,
furniture, vehicles, and more. The item must be something that can be physically
delivered to your address.

DealLock does not support digital goods, services, or subscription products.

---

## Which platforms are supported?

DealLock can automatically verify deals from:
- **Jumia** (jumia.com.ng)
- **Jiji** (jiji.ng)
- **Konga** (konga.com)

Deals from Instagram, Facebook Marketplace, and WhatsApp are accepted but require
manual review by the DealLock team because those platforms don't provide
verifiable seller data. Manual reviews typically take a few hours.

---

## How long does the AI review take?

For deals from Jumia, Jiji, or Konga: usually **2–5 minutes**.
For deals from Instagram, Facebook, or WhatsApp: **manual review**, usually
completed within a few hours during business hours.

You'll receive a notification on your dashboard and via SMS/email when the review
is complete.

---

## What does the AI check?

The AI engine runs three checks in parallel:

**Price Fairness** — it scrapes current prices for the same product on the
platform and compares your stated price to the market median. Results are:
Fair, Slightly Overpriced, Overpriced, or Suspiciously Low.

**Scam Probability** — it scores the seller based on available signals: seller
rating, cancellation rate, shipping speed, customer reviews, verification badge,
how long the listing has been active, and how many product photos are shown.

**Resellability** — it checks how frequently the same product is listed on the
platform and in what condition (new, fairly used, used). This tells DealLock how
easily the item could be resold if needed.

---

## Why was my deal rejected?

Common reasons for rejection:

- **Scam probability too high** — the seller's signals suggest significant fraud risk.
- **Price suspiciously low** — the price is far below market, which is a common
  sign of a bait-and-switch or non-delivery scam.
- **Item is illiquid** — no comparable listings were found on any platform,
  meaning DealLock cannot safely backstop the item.

If your deal was rejected, you'll receive a message explaining why. You're
welcome to submit a different deal or the same product from a different seller.

---

## What does "Flagged for review" mean?

A flagged deal is not rejected — it means the AI found some signals that need
a human to look at before a final decision is made. A DealLock team member will
review it and update the status usually within a few hours.

---

## How do installments work?

You choose either:
- **1 week** — you pay 50% up front and the remaining 50% within 7 days.
- **2 weeks (standard max)** — you pay 50% up front and the remaining balance in
  two equal weekly installments.

If you need more than 2 weeks, you can select "Request more than 2 weeks" on
the deal form. DealLock will review this manually based on the item and amount.

---

## What is my deposit?

Your deposit is the amount you already have on hand toward the product's price.
You enter this when submitting a deal. DealLock fronts the difference between
your deposit and the full price.

Example: product costs ₦150,000 and you have ₦50,000 — DealLock fronts
₦100,000 and you repay that amount plus any agreed service fee over your
installment period.

---

## Are there fees?

DealLock charges a service fee on the amount fronted. The exact fee is shown
to you before you confirm a deal. You will never be charged a fee that wasn't
disclosed upfront.
Customers bear all delivery costs. Both delivery to the Deallock warehouse where items are held in escrow and to the delivery address of the buyer.

---

## What happens if I can't repay on time?

Contact the DealLock support team before your repayment date. We can discuss
your situation. Late payments may affect your ability to submit future deals.
Default in payment completion without time extension would lead to your product being resold

---

## How do I track my deal?

Log in to your dashboard at any time. The deal status updates in real time:
Submitted → Under Review → Approved / Rejected → Secured → Delivered →
Repayment in Progress → Completed.

You'll also receive SMS and email notifications at key status changes.

---

## Is my data safe?

DealLock stores your personal and financial information securely. Seller contact
details you provide are used only for deal verification and are not shared with
third parties.

---

## How do I contact support?

- **In-app chat**: use the chat widget on your dashboard.
- **Email**: support@deallock.ng
- **WhatsApp**: available on the Contact page of the app.

Support hours are Monday–Saturday, 8 AM – 8 PM WAT.

---

## Can I cancel a deal?

You can cancel a deal before it is marked as Secured (i.e., before DealLock has
completed payment to the seller). Once a deal is Secured, cancellation is not
possible.
